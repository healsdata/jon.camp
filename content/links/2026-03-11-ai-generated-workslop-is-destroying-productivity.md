A BetterUp Labs and Stanford study found 41% of workers have encountered AI-generated "workslop" (output that looks polished but lacks substance), costing nearly two hours of rework per instance. Despite AI adoption doubling since 2023, 95% of organizations report no measurable ROI. The authors argue leaders need to model purposeful AI use and set clear quality standards rather than issuing blanket adoption mandates.

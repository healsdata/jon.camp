Steve Blank argues that startups founded before 2025 are likely operating from obsolete assumptions. The rapid shift to AI-driven development, autonomous agents replacing traditional interfaces, and dramatically changed cost structures have upended startup economics. Founders need to reassess their technical stacks, team structures, and pricing models or risk building on a dead foundation.

AI coding tools are flattening traditional software team hierarchies, creating tension as engineers, PMs, designers, and leaders all believe they can now perform each other's roles. The author calls this dynamic a "Mexican standoff" and argues that collaborative, AI-driven pair programming is the solution where diverse expertise remains valuable even as implementation barriers lower.

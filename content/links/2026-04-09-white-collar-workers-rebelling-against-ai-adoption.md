Roughly 80% of enterprise workers are avoiding or outright ignoring employer mandates to adopt AI tools. Workers cite inadequate training and fears about job displacement, while many AI tools fail to deliver the productivity gains companies are promising. The piece frames this as a trust gap between executives invested in AI rollouts and employees who remain skeptical of the technology.

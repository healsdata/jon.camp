The author draws a parallel between cold reading techniques used by psychics and how chat-based LLMs create an illusion of intelligence. Users perceive specific, insightful responses when models are actually producing statistically generic outputs, and cognitive biases do the rest of the work. RLHF training reinforces this by optimizing for the feeling of correctness rather than actual correctness.

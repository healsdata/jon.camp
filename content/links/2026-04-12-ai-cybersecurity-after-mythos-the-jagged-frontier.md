AISLE researchers tested Anthropic's Mythos vulnerability-detection claims by running smaller open-source models through the same tasks and found the gap much smaller than expected. Their conclusion is that orchestration and system design matter more than raw model size, and that cybersecurity capability doesn't scale as cleanly with model power as Anthropic's framing implies.

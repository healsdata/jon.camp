Thoughtworks CTO Rachel Laycock shares takeaways from a February 2026 industry retreat in Utah, where practitioners discussed how AI adoption is changing day-to-day engineering work. Topics ranged from the cognitive load of supervising AI agents to shifting staff roles, evolving code review practices, and the emergence of self-healing systems.

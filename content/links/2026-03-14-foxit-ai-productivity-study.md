A Foxit report found that while 89% of executives claim AI improves productivity, actual measured gains tell a different story. Executives net just 16 minutes per week after factoring in verification time, while end users lose 14 minutes on net. Foxit calls this the "verification burden" where time saved generating content gets consumed reviewing and validating it.

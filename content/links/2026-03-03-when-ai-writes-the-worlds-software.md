As AI generates 25-30% of code at major tech companies, formal mathematical verification becomes critical. Testing alone cannot guarantee correctness at AI-generation speeds. The author proposes Lean as a verification platform for building a provably correct software stack, particularly for cryptography, databases, and critical infrastructure.

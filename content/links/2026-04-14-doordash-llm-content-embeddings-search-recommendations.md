DoorDash describes how they replaced sparse metadata with LLM-generated merchant and item profiles to produce richer content embeddings for search and recommendations across food, grocery, and gifting verticals. Their key finding: input data quality matters far more than model choice — switching from raw metadata to LLM profiles yielded +31% hit rate improvement, while switching models on the same raw data yielded only +6%. The content-first approach reduced null search rate by 3.65%, lifted search conversion, and powers generative personalized homepage carousels that improved order rate by 2.4%.

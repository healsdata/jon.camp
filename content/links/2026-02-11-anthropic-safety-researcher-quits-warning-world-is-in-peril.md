Mrinank Sharma, an Anthropic safety researcher, resigned citing concerns that the safety team "constantly [faces] pressures to set aside what matters most." He warns about AI risks including bioterrorism. This follows similar departures at other AI firms, reflecting ongoing tensions between rapid AI development and safety considerations.

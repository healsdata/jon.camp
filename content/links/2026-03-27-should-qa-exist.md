Jade Rubick takes a nuanced look at whether engineering teams need dedicated QA. He mostly agrees with the prevailing view that they don't, but argues that where QA does exist it should be embedded, automation-focused, and handoff-free. He also sketches a speculative "Automated Verification Engineer" role aimed at making quality work high-leverage in the age of AI-assisted development.

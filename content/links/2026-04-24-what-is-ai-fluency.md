Introducing "AI Fluency" as the judgment to collaborate effectively with AI, not just knowing which buttons to click. The 4D Framework, developed by researchers at Ringling College and University College Cork, identifies four core competencies for working well with AI: Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence.

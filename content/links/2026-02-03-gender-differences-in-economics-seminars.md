---
title: "Gender Differences in Economics Seminars"
date: 2026-02-03
published: 2026-02-03
link: "https://www.aeaweb.org/articles?id=10.1257/aer.20241718"
---

Research analyzing interruption patterns across thousands of economics seminars finds that women are interrupted more than men and face more negative or cutting-off interruptions. Female audience members showed greater engagement with female presenters, suggesting a potential mentorship effect in academic economics.

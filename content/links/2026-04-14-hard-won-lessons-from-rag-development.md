A practical guide to production RAG systems, based on extensive hands-on experience. Covers document-based chunking with context preservation, late chunking for embeddings, hierarchical search with hybrid scoring, HyDE for query enhancement, and self-reflective retrieval. The core argument is that good enterprise AI comes from thoughtful combinations of proven techniques, not any single breakthrough.

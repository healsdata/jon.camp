Argues that organizations should treat AI as an exoskeleton for human capability rather than as a replacement worker. AI amplifies competent people by handling repetitive and error-prone tasks, preserving cognitive resources for creative judgment. The enduring value of AI will come from tools so well-integrated into human workflows that they feel like natural extensions of human capability.

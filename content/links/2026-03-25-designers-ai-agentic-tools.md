As AI tools accelerate implementation speed, the bottleneck in software development shifts from building to figuring out what to build. This piece argues that designers need to add skills like prompt engineering and context architecture to their practice. The underlying judgment and taste required to specify intent for autonomous systems remains a human responsibility.

Dax Raad, the developer behind SST and OpenCode, posted a viral six-bullet tweet that Reading.sh calls "a cold, clinical autopsy of what AI is actually doing to software teams." His core argument is that feeling productive with AI is not the same as being productive. Sometimes doing things manually is faster, and well-organized codebases matter more than fancy multi-agent setups.

Addy Osmani outlines a disciplined approach to AI-assisted programming. Rather than treating LLMs as autonomous coders, he emphasizes planning specs first, breaking work into small chunks, providing extensive context, and staying in the review loop. The human engineer remains the director while AI accelerates mechanical tasks.

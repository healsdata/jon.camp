Addy Osmani introduces "Agentic Engine Optimization" as a new discipline for structuring docs so AI coding agents can actually use them. Agents fetch content in single requests, ignore visual design, and hit token limits, so the piece advocates for `llms.txt` discoverability, clear capability signaling, token-efficient content, and separate AI traffic analytics.

Laurent Domb (SVP at Mastercard) argues that AI has flipped the development bottleneck: implementation is now cheap, but coherence and judgment are scarce. Developers end up chasing AI output rather than directing it, producing code that ships faster but reverts more and coheres less. He proposes redesigned SDLC and PDLC pipelines with explicit problem framing, decomposition phases, and continuous validation to keep each role directing at the right altitude.

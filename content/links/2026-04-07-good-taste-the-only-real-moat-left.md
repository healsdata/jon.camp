When competent output is cheap, the scarce skill shifts to rejection - knowing what is too generic rather than just what is acceptable. But the article warns against reducing humans to selectors at the end of a machine-driven pipeline, arguing that curation without authorship is a narrow and fragile role. The real edge comes from applying specific context, constraints, and consequences that the model cannot supply on its own.

AI-assisted coding offers real productivity gains, but over-reliance risks cognitive atrophy and the erosion of senior-level skills. The right amount of AI usage is somewhere between zero and maximum, requiring developers to stay mentally engaged rather than fully delegating tasks. Using AI adoption as a performance metric backfires by incentivizing gaming over genuine productivity.

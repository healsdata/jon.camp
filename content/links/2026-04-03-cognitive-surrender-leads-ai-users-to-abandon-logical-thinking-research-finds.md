---
title: '"Cognitive surrender" leads AI users to abandon logical thinking, research finds'
date: 2026-04-03T00:00:00-05:00
published: 2026-04-03T00:00:00-05:00
link: "https://arstechnica.com/ai/2026/04/research-finds-ai-users-scarily-willing-to-surrender-their-cognition-to-llms/"
---

University of Pennsylvania researchers gave participants access to an LLM that deliberately gave wrong answers half the time, then measured how often people caught the errors. Across 1,372 participants, subjects accepted faulty AI reasoning 73 percent of the time and only overruled it about 20 percent of the time. Time pressure made things worse; financial incentives and immediate feedback helped, but not by much.

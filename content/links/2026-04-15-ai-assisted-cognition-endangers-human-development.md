Leaning on AI for thinking risks human development through "AI-skew", the tendency of large language models to perpetuate outdated patterns from their training data. The author argues this narrows the diversity of ideas needed for cultural and scientific progress, functioning like "cognitive inbreeding." Proposed mitigations include consulting multiple AI models and prioritizing human discussion, though measuring these harms at a population level remains an open problem.

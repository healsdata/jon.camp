Simon Wardley identifies four types of debt that accumulate in systems beyond the familiar technical debt. He frames evolutionary debt (outdated positioning in the value chain), cognitive debt (flawed reasoning models), and epistemic debt (gaps in understanding) as equally important concerns. Each type compounds over time and requires different strategies to address.

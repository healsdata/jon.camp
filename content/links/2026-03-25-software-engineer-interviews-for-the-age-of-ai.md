Swizec Teller argues that good engineers have "fractal knowledge" that holds up at any depth, while leetcode interviews only test surface-level pattern matching. He makes the case that interviews should give candidates real problems and observe how far their understanding extends. With AI handling rote implementation, the gap between deep and shallow knowledge only gets harder to hide.

Pushback on the "built in 24 hours with AI" narrative. The author built a daily puzzle game and found that the upfront product thinking -- paper testing, rules validation, clear documentation -- mattered far more than coding speed. AI helped stress-test the game logic, but it couldn't substitute for doing the design work first.

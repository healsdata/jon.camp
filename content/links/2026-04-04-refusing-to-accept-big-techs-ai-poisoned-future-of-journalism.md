Marisa Kabas argues that AI-generated content degrades journalism at its core, and that the craft depends on human judgment, ethical responsibility, and the willingness to struggle with hard material in ways machines cannot replicate. She criticizes outlets using AI for story production and frames resistance as a matter of professional integrity.

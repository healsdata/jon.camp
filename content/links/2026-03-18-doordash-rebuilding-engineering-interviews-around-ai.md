DoorDash is replacing algorithmic coding rounds with 60-minute AI-assisted working sessions on realistic starter projects. Candidates use their own machine, their preferred IDE, and whatever AI tools they want -- the interview scores orientation speed, tool use and verification, debugging, scope management, and communication. The piece is honest about what didn't work in pilots, including projects that were too large and candidates who showed up without AI tools configured because they assumed using them was cheating.

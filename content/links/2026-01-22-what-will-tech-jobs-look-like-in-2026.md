The tech industry faces contradictory trends in 2026: companies are cutting staff while claiming AI will enable "doing more with less," yet deployment remains limited. Workers demand hybrid arrangements while employers push office returns. New specialized AI-focused job titles are emerging as roles narrow from broad categories like "software engineer."

title: "Largest study of its kind shows AI assistants misrepresent news content 45% of the time"
date: 2026-04-04T00:00:00-05:00
published: 2025-10-22T00:00:00-05:00
link: "https://www.bbc.co.uk/mediacentre/2025/new-ebu-research-ai-assistants-news-content"
---

22 public service media organizations across 18 countries tested ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, and Perplexity against more than 3,000 news-related queries. 45% of responses had at least one significant issue, 31% had sourcing problems, and 20% contained major accuracy errors including hallucinated details. Gemini performed worst, with significant issues in 76% of responses. The EBU is now pressing EU and national regulators to enforce existing information integrity laws.

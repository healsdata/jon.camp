John Arundel introduces a six-step workflow for working with Junie, JetBrains' AI coding agent, in GoLand. The acronym PIRATE covers Plan, Iterate, Review, Assess, Test, and Evaluate, with each step illustrated by building a small calculator program. The focus throughout is on keeping human judgment in the loop rather than rubber-stamping AI output.

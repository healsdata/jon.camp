As AI tools raise the throughput ceiling for engineering teams, leaders need to explicitly reset what "good work" looks like. The post argues for "intentional adoption", meaning engineers who judge when AI accelerates the work and when deep focus matters more, rather than chasing raw velocity. Quality standards should hold even as output speeds up, and unchecked backlog growth can become a new source of burnout.

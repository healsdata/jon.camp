A CLI tool that plays game character voice lines when AI coding agents need your attention. It hooks into Claude Code, Cursor, and other IDEs to provide audio and desktop notifications for events like task completion and permission requests. Supports 43+ sound packs from games like Warcraft and Portal, with remote development support for SSH and containers.

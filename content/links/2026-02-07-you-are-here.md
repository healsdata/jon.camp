Marc Brooker explores technology's future as AI and automation reduce coding costs. He presents two paths: clinging to traditional programming as a craft, or embracing new opportunities in systems integration and problem-solving. He argues the cost of building reliable, secure end-to-end systems is dropping, positioning this as software's pivotal second act.

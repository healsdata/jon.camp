title: "Grammarly Is Pulling Down Its Explosively Controversial Feature That Impersonates Writers Without Their Permission"
date: 2026-03-11T12:00:00-05:00
published: 2026-03-11T14:18:00-05:00
link: "https://futurism.com/artificial-intelligence/grammarly-pulls-down-expert-review-feature"
---

Grammarly's "Expert Review" feature let users get writing suggestions supposedly inspired by real writers and experts, without those people's consent. After backlash from journalists including Kara Swisher, parent company Superhuman disabled the feature. CEO Shishir Mehrotra apologized and said they'll reimagine it with real opt-in control for the experts involved.

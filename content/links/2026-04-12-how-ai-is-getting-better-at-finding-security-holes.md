NPR talks to open-source maintainers and security researchers about the ground-level shift they've seen in AI-generated bug reports in early 2026. cURL's Daniel Stenberg describes going from a flood of AI-generated slop reports in 2025 to mostly legitimate finds this year. The piece frames Anthropic's Mythos Preview and Project Glasswing as a controlled attempt to give defenders a head start before these capabilities spread.
